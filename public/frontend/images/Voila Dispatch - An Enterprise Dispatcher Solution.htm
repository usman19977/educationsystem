C) Features - top

Below are some of the great features of the app.

Features included in Dispatcher Panel
  1. Unique Dispatch Algorithm - Completely customised algorithm for dispatcher to calculate prices automatically along with auto dispatch mechanism.
  2. Attractive Designs - Stylish UI with everything accessible on a single page with one click.
  3. Realtime Partner’s Tracking - All partner’s who installed our mobile app will be tracked in real time on a map.
  4. Partner Zone Queue - Partner’s are queued to dispatcher on the basis of their completion time, along with real time zone queue updates.
  5. Realtime Event’s - All booking’s listing along with their event’s are updated on real-time with sound notifications and colour’s.
  6. Job Colors - Colorize job’s on the basis of assigning time and status along with sound’s notification to make them stand out.
  7. Distributed Databases - Database is designed to function in cluster flawlessly and also used multiple databases ( MongoDB & MySql ).
  8. 100% Scalable - Massively scalable, Rock solid performance, designed for speed at great price point.
  9. Multiple Dispatcher Assignment’s - System is designed to work on Manual and automatic assignment.
  10. Recurring Jobs - Dispatcher can create recurring jobs on the basis of days of the week and end dates as well.
  11. Zone Tariffs - Tariffs can be set as per Zone’s, We also offer customisation for tariffs and calculation edits as per your request.
  12. Partner Event’s - Entire order event’s starting from creation, assignment to completion will be visible with their date and time.
  13. Unique and Stylish Booking Interface - Clean UX with Stylish designed for dispatchers to create booking in seconds along with wow animation’s.
  14. Filter & Search Job’s - Dispatcher can easily search booking’s with lots of search filter options along with entire job details and event history.
  15. Close Job - Dispatcher can view all job’s along with an option to edit and copy the same.
  16. Driver’s Real Time analysis - Dispatcher can view all driver’s statics along with their wait time, free time, today’s earning, on duty timings etc,.
  17. Upcoming Job History - Dispatcher can view all schedule jobs with pagination options.
  18. Unlimited Booking’s - Unlimited booking’s created and dispatched with multiple dispatcher option’s .
  19. Fare Estimation - Dispatcher can get fare estimation and ETA before making booking’s.
  20. Animation’s- Cool animations and effects to get great dispatch experience.
New & Noteworthy features of Partner App

Another side END POINT of the dispatch system is partner App and it comes packed with great features which make dispatch experience unique.
Powered by by Native Technologies

  1. 100% Native Android Code - Coded with pure android native java code and material design UI.
  2. Miss call verification - Integrated with checkmobi.com miss call verification service.
  3. On Duty & Off Duty - Partners can turn on-Duty to get more jobs and off-Duty in case they need rest.
  4. Commision Calculation - Commision will be calculated and accordingly driver & company’s earnings are calculated.
  5. Stylish Popup and Cool animation’s - Stylish Popups and alerts in app’s with clean UX along with cool animations to make great user experience.
  6. Job Queue - SDispatchers can send multiple job’s to partner’s and are queued and will be completed as per partner’s choice.
  7. Up-comming and History Booking - All pending booking will appear under upcoming booking along with status and completed bookings are available in history.
  8. Event Colour - Right from Partner Accept, Arrived, Begin Job, Job Completed are all marked with unique colors in both Partner app and dispatch to make it stand out.
  9. Future Jobs and Reserve - Drivers can also search for Future Job’s and assign jobs to themselves.
  10. Driver Earnings and statistics - Beautifully laid out statics in tabular and graphical format with an option to filter by date.
